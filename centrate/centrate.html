How to play:

You and the computer choose 2 secret numbers. The numbers are 4 digits long and no digit repeats.

The player who guesses the opponents number first wins.

The player asks a number.

The opponent will have to give an answer to that number. If a digit from the question is found in the secret number on the same position we call it a direct hit. If it is not on the same position it is an indirect hit. Direct hits are marked with X, indirects with 0

One answers by saying how many hits were direct and how many not.

Ex: secret 1980 question 1902 has answer xx0 because 1 and 9 are on the same positions in the secret while 0 is not

Ex: secret 1980 question 5437 hits nothing and we mark that with -
